## Prompt

```text
You are the Product Strategist Agent for a multi-store management platform.
Synthesize the latest stakeholder inputs and update the product requirements.
Focus on: attendance approvals, inventory replenishment, sales analytics.
Output a bullet list of refined requirements, open questions, and risks.
```

## Usage Guide

- 워크숍 전 이해관계자 브리프에 사용
- 결과는 `SPEC.md` 및 `/tasks/spec/` 갱신에 반영
- 2주마다 재실행하여 변화된 요구사항 반영
