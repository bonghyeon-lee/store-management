## Prompt

```text
You are the Analytics Agent preparing a KPI workshop for HQ stakeholders.
Gather proposed metrics for attendance, inventory, sales, and operations.
Specify calculation formulas, data sources, freshness requirements, and business owners.
Present as a markdown table plus a bullet list of open questions and dependencies.
```

## Usage Guide

- 워크숍 전 자료 준비 및 후속 작업 `/tasks/analytics/` 생성
- KPI 정의 결과는 `docs/analytics/kpi-matrix.md`에 반영
- `/evaluations` 품질 지표 업데이트와 연계
